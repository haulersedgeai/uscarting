## 4. CORE BUSINESS TYPE — CONFIRMED

**Hybrid: Roll-off dumpster rental + Full-service junk removal** (residential + light commercial / contractor).

Slogan: "Dumpster Rentals & Junk Removal at your fingertips"
"24/7 junk removal and roll-off dumpster rental services in Westchester and Fairfield County"

Not a commercial scheduled-pickup carting operation (no front-load contracts, compactors, restaurant-route language). The "carting" in the name refers to hauling — they primarily compete with brands like College Hunks / 1-800-Got-Junk for junk removal and with local roll-off rental companies. Strong contractor/renovation customer base (one review: "contractor in Fairfield County… working with US Carting for 5 years").

### Junk Removal — Full Service
4-step process: (1) Make an appointment, (2) Fair pricing (all-in quote), (3) We haul, (4) Responsible disposal (recycling/donations + tax-deductible receipt).

**What they take (Household)**: Old appliances, refrigerators & freezers, mattresses, box springs, furniture (couches & sofas), TV recycling, tire recycling, hot-tub demolition & removal.

**What they take (Office)**: Computer / monitor / printer / hard-drive recycling, office chairs, conference tables, cubicle breakdown, large desks & credenzas, file cabinets.

**Clean-outs**: Attic, basement, garage, shed, shed demo, estate clean-outs, restoration / renovation cleanup, foreclosure junk removal.

### Other Services
- **Grizzly Bags** — branded dumpster-bag offering ("Buy It | You Bag It | We'll Grab It"); ideal for homeowners/contractors, no rental period
- **Demolition** — light demolition + debris removal in one trip

### What they DON'T take
- Anything with gas or oil
- Medical, biological waste, feces
- Asbestos
- Boats and vehicles
- Chemicals, pesticides
- Oil furnaces (even empty tanks)
- Wet paint cans
- Propane tanks (loaded or empty)
- Rocks, cement, dirt
